How we work

An education rooted in respect & wonder

Our goal is to provide each child with the opportunity to reach their full potential — intellectually, physically, socially, and emotionally — regardless of gender, ethnicity, or preexisting abilities.

Musical, physical, and creative activities complement our programme, alongside festivals from different cultures, museum visits, and seasonal activities. Great importance is attached to caring interaction, both between adults and children, and among the children themselves.

On Wednesdays, children visit the physical education course of KISS at TSV Ost München (except during school holidays).

Holistic Learning

Experiencing and learning with all of the senses. Mind, feelings, sensory perception, and movement form a harmony — their joint activation develops the child's personality and awakens the desire to discover the world.

Independence

The child's need for spontaneous learning is taken seriously. From an early age, children learn to tie their own shoelaces, set the table, and take care of personal responsibilities. Help me to do it myself!

Individuality

Each child has their own developmental profile, personal strengths, and character. Recognising and strengthening these individual characteristics — while learning to appreciate the abilities of others — is our continuous endeavour.

The Prepared Environment

We provide Montessori developmental materials that enable each child to appropriate the world in an autonomous and individual manner. The environment itself is designed to invite exploration and discovery.

Cooperation with Parents

Parent–teacher assemblies meet five times a year. All parents take an active role — from weekly shopping and laundry to helping out when a staff member is away. It is a true community endeavour.

About us

A multicultural team, one shared mission

Baobab is a multicultural parents' initiative, led by a team of qualified Montessori teachers who bring a rich diversity of backgrounds and native languages to the classroom every day.

The parents are organised in the cooperative Baobab e.V., based at Zeppelinstraße 69, 81669 München. The kindergarten has offered 19 places and has been supported by the state capital city Munich (Referat für Bildung und Sport) since 1996.

The team is supported by a trainee or Federal Volunteer Service participant, who joins us on a yearly basis.
